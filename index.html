Introduction

Our Services

Kitchen Delights takes pride in making the most tantalizing, mouth-watering foods from all over the world using different recipes gathered by us on this application. Our skilled and most sort after chefs have over 10 years curlinary experience in world famous hotel restaurants like Hotel Intercontinental and The Mariot Hotel.

Why Kitchen Delights?

We have taken up the load of making things way easier for you, the busy person. Anything on the recipes that interests you and would like to sample it but you just don't have the time? Worry not! We've got you.

What to do?

  1. Scroll through our menu or search through the various recipes we have on our platform.
  2. Identify that meal (or meals, we don't judge ;-)) that stir up your cravings.
  3. Place your order through the channel below.
  4. Leave your contact and location details.
  5. Your meal shall be delivered as soon as it is ready by our trusted team of efficient delivery personel.
  6. Payment is made on delivery by either cash-on-hand or via MPESA (Tel.No: +254700001122)
